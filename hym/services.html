    Services

    The HYM Investment Group, LLC develops, invests, owns and manages real estate assets for its own portfolio and on behalf of major institutional and private investors. Asset classes include:

    • Urban Mixed-Use
    • Commercial Office
    • Lab/Biotech
    • Apartments & Condominiums
    • Academic, Hospital and Institutional Retail

    In addition, HYM offers a full complement of real estate based services for Commercial, Retail and Multifamily Assets including:

    • Project Development and Development Advisory Services (Including Due Diligence, Permits & Approvals, Design Management and Construction Oversight)
    • Property Management Services
    • Turnkey Tenant Build-outs and Owner Representation Services
    • Asset Management Services
    • Finance and Accounting Services
